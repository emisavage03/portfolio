Good morning world! I am Emi Savage. I am a media artist and undergrad student based in Los Angeles. By blending emerging technology and sensory experience, I make work that draws you in to the body, the web, and the things in your backyard. My disciplines include creative code, projection, installation, graphic design, and video.

I am currently attending the Media Arts + Practice program at the University of Southern California. In my free time, I like to collect CDs and feel the sun on my back.
