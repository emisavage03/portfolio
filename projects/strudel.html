Strudel Workshop

February 2026

Strudel is a live coding environment for algorithmic music patterns. For this project, I partnered with ZAP to lead a workshop teaching Strudel live coding to my peers.

I prepared a cover of Headlock by Imogen Heap by referencing this sheet music and converting it to Strudel code. During the workshop, I explained the basics of the language and demoed each instrument line by line. At the end of the workshop, participants had a working copy of the code.
