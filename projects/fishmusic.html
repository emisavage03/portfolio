Fish Music

Fall 2024

I created this collection of p5.js sketches when learning to code. Each explores various elements of code and interactivity that helped me understand the possibilities of creative code and fostered my love for the medium.

Fish Music is a coded music game where players create a piece of music by interacting with the fish in the sketch. I coded and illustrated this piece, and the music was provided by a close friend.


Kiki+Bouba is a looping coded music video to Noise is a Social Skill by Sweet Trip. Each frame was illustrated by me.


Fish Out of Water is a coded poetry environment of a haiku I wrote. Navigate the 3D WebGL sketch to read the entire piece. I also created the fish model.
