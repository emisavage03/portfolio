DisZAPster

January 2025

DisZAPster is a card game created for Zapathon 2025, an annual student-run 24hr media-making marathon. In following the event's prompt of "RBG," I collaborated with teammates Isabel Fowlkes, Kyler Caldwell, and Kailin Xia to rapid prototype a printing/lighting game mechanic that omitted certain information on the cards when shown in red, green, or blue LED lighting. We also built a lit and covered structure to play the game inside, and at the end of the festival we demoed the game to the audience of attendees.
