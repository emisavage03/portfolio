Lamp Show Doc

November 2025

In this project, my collaborators Akasa Mei, Nina Tomasevic, and I visited AUTOBODY autobody, an intimate Los Angeles art gallery, to document their Lamp Show exhibition.

We created an 8 minute short film exploring the craft of both the artists and the AUTOBODY autobody curators. My roles in this documentary included directing, editing, and sound and music.

You can view the film on Vimeo.
