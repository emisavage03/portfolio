Heavy Traffic

Fall 2025

In this project, I reimagined the Los Angeles sound environment through a sonic installation built on live traffic camera data and an arrangement of field recordings from the South Central area. I was interested in the intersection of urban sound and surveillance, and what it means for the flow of people and vehicles to replace the flow of water in the Los Angeles watershed.

I composed the field recording arrangement in Adobe Audition. Once completed, I imported the composition into a TouchDesigner project, where I used the webBrowser interface to display a live traffic camera off the 710 and I-10. I then used the open-source object detection algorithm TDYolo to detect vehicles in each frame. Each vehicle detection triggered notes from a virtual MIDI player using loopMIDI and Decent Sampler. I layered the MIDI notes over the field recording arrangement to get the final piece.
