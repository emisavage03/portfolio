Pinterest Art Account

2021-2023

I posted weekly sketchbook videos to my Pinterest account for nearly two years, earning over 18K followers and monetizing my profile through the now-defunct Pinterest Creator Rewards Program. I enjoyed sharing my progress to a tight community of artists and fans, also while practicing documenting my growth.

While the account no longer exists, an archived version of my page from Nov 2022 is available on the Internet Archive.
